Wilkinson

Mark Wilkinson, better known by his stage name "Wilkinson", is an English record producer, DJ and remixer from Hammersmith, West London.
Wilkinson has released music on RAM Records and Hospital Records, as well as Virgin EMI.
His 2013 single "Afterglow" with vocals from Becky Hill reached number 8 on the UK Singles Chart.

Biography

Dimension

Robert Etheridge, better known by his stage name "Dimension", is an English DJ, record producer, songwriter and sound engineer from London, England.
Dimension has been releasing records since 2012, and in March 2021, Dimension released his debut album "Organ".
He has previously collaborated with other producers, including "Wilkinson" and "Sub Focus".

Biography

Sub Focus

Nicolaas Douwma, better known by his stage name "Sub Focus", is an English DJ, record producer, songwriter, musician and sound engineer from Guildford, Surrey.
He has been releasing records since 2003. In October 2009, he released his self-titled debut album. In September 2013, he released his second album "Torus".
Douwma's album in collaboration with "Wilkinson" titled "Portals" was released in October 2020 through Casablanca Records.

Biography

Culture Shock

James Pountney, better known by his stage name "Culture Shock", is an English drum and bass DJ and record producer hailing from London, England.
In 2004 he released "The Vega EP" on the Moving Shadow record label.
Two years later after attracting a lot of attention he signed with RAM Records under which he released his first EP titled "The Third Stage".
