Wilkinson

Mark Wilkinson, better known by his stage name "Wilkinson", is an English record producer, DJ and remixer from Hammersmith, West London. Wilkinson has released music on RAM records and Hospital Records, as well as Virgin EMI. His 2013 single "Afterglow" with vocals from Becky Hill reached number 8 on the UK Singles Chart.
